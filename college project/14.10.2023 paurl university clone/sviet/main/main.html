Chandigarh - The City Beautiful

Explore

You can be something of a globetrotter, exploring the realms of nature near the SVGOI campus. Situated near Chandigarh, surrounded by the green fields of Punjab SVGOI offers a life surrounded by cultural activities and modern infrastructure to be explored. From the world renounced heritage Rock Garden to the beautiful evening scenes of the Sukhna Lake, explore the masterpiece of famous Swiss-French architecture by le Corbusier.

Well Connected

SVGOI’s Campus is well connected with a 70ft highway which makes it easily accessible from Chandigarh Airport and Railway station as well. The well-maintained roads leading to the SVGOI campus make it easy for students to enjoy student life while following their passion.

Business

SVGOI has emerged as north India’s favorite college to begin a career in the IT and service industry. Many opportunities are offered by the faculties during campus placement drives. Students can build their successful careers in the field of their choice at SVGOI.
